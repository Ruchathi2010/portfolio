Academic Integrity Policy — Please Read Before Booking
This tutoring service is strictly focused on conceptual understanding, skill development, and guided learning. I help students genuinely understand their coursework so they can perform independently and confidently.

I do not write assignments, complete coursework, or sit exams on behalf of students. Requests for this type of assistance will be declined without exception. This is a firm, non-negotiable policy — both as a matter of professional ethics and out of respect for the academic institutions my students attend.

If your goal is to genuinely learn and build skills that will serve you throughout your career — you are in exactly the right place.
